## Section 4: Psychological Screening & Contraindications

### 4.1 Purpose of This Section

The TTAO Inner Academy curriculum involves deep inner work, including shadow integration, visualization, and engagement with unconscious material. This work can be profoundly transformative, but it is not appropriate for everyone at all times.

This section asks about psychological and medical factors that may indicate a need for modified participation, additional support, or temporary pause in enrollment. **Your answers will not automatically disqualify you.** They help the Headmaster make informed decisions about how to support you safely.

---

### ⚠️ **CRISIS RESOURCES – PLEASE READ BEFORE CONTINUING**

**If you are currently experiencing suicidal ideation, active self-harm urges, or acute psychological crisis, please contact a crisis line before completing this form.** Your safety is the priority.

**Crisis resources by country:**

- **United States:** 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988) | [988lifeline.org](https://988lifeline.org)
- **United Kingdom:** Samaritans 116 123 | [samaritans.org](https://www.samaritans.org)
- **Canada:** Talk Suicide Canada 1-833-456-4566 | [talksuicide.ca](https://www.talksuicide.ca)
- **Australia:** Lifeline 13 11 14 | [lifeline.org.au](https://www.lifeline.org.au)
- **EU-wide:** 116 123 (Emotional Support Helpline, available in multiple languages)
- **International Directory:** [findahelpline.com](https://findahelpline.com)

If you are in immediate danger, please contact emergency services in your country (911, 999, 112, etc.).

## Document Retention & Privacy Policy

All intake forms are retained securely by the Headmaster for the duration of the student's enrollment and for **7 years** following withdrawal or completion, in accordance with standard educational record-keeping practices.

Intake information is shared only with the student's assigned Guide(s) on a need-to-know basis. It is never shared with other students or external parties without the student's explicit written consent, except as required by law (e.g., mandatory reporting of child abuse, imminent threat to self or others).

Students may request a copy of their intake form at any time by contacting the Headmaster.
